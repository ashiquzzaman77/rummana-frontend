About Us

We Are In A Mission To Help The Helpless

Our Mission

Prioritize mental health well-being among the student population. Raise awareness, break the stigma, and introduce healthy coping mechanisms.

Vision

We want to create a society where all students have access to the mental health resources they need to thrive.

Who we are

Project Rummana is the first non-profit organization to focus on student’s mental health well-being in Bangladesh

Why now?

More adolescent adults are now depressed than ever with growing rates of suicide. In fact, mental health related deaths are currently the most common form of death among young adults in Bangladesh.

How we do it

Did you know, research by Dhaka Medical College shows that 2 hours of Project Rummana’s workshop has shown effective long-lasting positive results?

Services

Our Services

Workshops

We offer to raise awareness and equip groups of students with skills in our classroom workshops with topics on stress management, anxiety management, depression and grief

1-on-1 psychotherapy

We offer individual psychotherapy to help students address mental health challenges tailored to their needs

Long-term mental health counseling

We offer long term mental health council to help students from Depression, Anxiety, Stress and make long lasting positive impact in their life
